Sathish Babu

Sri. Sathish Babu was born into a music family in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. His grandfather was a Carnatic classical and Bhajan singer. He started learning Tabla from the age of 15.

Sri. Sathish Babu respectfully dedicates his career to his Gurus,
Most revered Tabla Legend Ustad Alla Rakha Khan Saheb,
Prof. Manohar. N.Keskar ( Poular Tabla Artist and Teacher at 'Tansen Sur Sangam' School of Music, TVM),
Pandit Ramesh Narayan ( disciple of Pt.Jasrajji, also a renowned Vocalist and Music Director), and
Sri. Sreedharan Master ( renowned recording Artist from Calicut, Kerala ).

From the last 31 years Sri. Sathish Babu has been pursuing a career in Tabla Music, performing Solo Concerts and accompanying for popular Hindusthani artists in India and abroad. He had the great opportunity to accompany -

Sangeeth Marthand Pandit Jasrajji.
Pandit Vinayak Torviji
Pandit Ramesh Narayan
